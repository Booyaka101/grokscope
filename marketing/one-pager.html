Why it's different

  • Live, not indexed — Grok 4.5's server-side x_search reads X in real time; no stale SEO content.
  • Every claim cited — inline numbered citations, rendered as clickable terminal hyperlinks (OSC 8).
  • Recency tags — post dates decoded from X snowflake IDs: you see (4d ago), not "recently".
  • Filters & automation — --handles, --exclude, --days; --json for CI, --md for newsletters, doctor for setup checks.
  • BYOK = private & cheap — your xAI key, your data; exact cost printed after every query (typically a few cents).
